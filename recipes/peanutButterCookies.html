Peanut Butter Cookies

These incredible 5 ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies will change your life. No, I'm not kidding. Your life will be forever altered by the introduction of these cookies.   • Prep Time: 10 Minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter Not jumbo!
  • 1/4 cup stevia sweetener
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • Optional: Other spices

Recpie

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mix peanut butter, stevia, egg, cocoa powder, and vanilla with an electric mixer or in a food processor until well combined. This is also when you can add any other spices. Roll dough into sixteen 1-inch balls. Place 1 to 2 inches apart onto an ungreased baking sheet. Flatten balls with a fork.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are set, about 10 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool on the baking sheet for 10 minutes.
  4. Line a tray with waxed paper. Carefully transfer cookies to the tray and let cool to room temperature, about 15 minutes. Refrigerate until fully set, about 30 minutes.
