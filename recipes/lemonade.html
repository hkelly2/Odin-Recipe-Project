Lemonade Recipe

The creature hates this stuff, but I can't get enough. It would rather drink pig's blood and lard. What a loser! This stuff really will change your life. I have been making batches of this in between making cookies for the creature thing and I have had to scale this recipe up 4 times to match the amount of it I consume. Get ready for your new vice.

  • Prep Time: 10 Minutes
  • Cook Time: 5 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 9 medium lemons, or more as needed Not jumbo!
  • 7 cups ice-cold water
  • ice as needed
  • Optional: Other spices

Recpie

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine sugar and 1 cup water in a small saucepan. Stir to dissolve sugar while mixture comes to a boil. Set aside to cool slightly.
  3. Meanwhile, roll lemons around on your counter to soften. Cut in half crosswise, and squeeze into a liquid measuring cup. Add pulp to the juice, but discard any seeds. Continue juicing until you have 1 Â½ cups fresh juice and pulp.
  4. Pour 7 cups ice-cold water into a pitcher. This is also when you can add any other spices. Stir in lemon juice and pulp, then add simple syrup to taste. Add ice.
  5. Enjoy!
